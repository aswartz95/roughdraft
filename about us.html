About Us

Welcome to our small family-owned Boer Goat business! Over the past six years, we have dedicated ourselves to building a top-quality breeding program, producing exceptional Boer goat stock. Our commitment extends beyond just sales; we deeply value the relationships we form wih our buyers and fellow breeders, often leading to lifelong friendships. We are passionate about offering the best possible experience for our showmen. On our website, you will find detailed information about the goats available for sale or lease, the variety of services we offer, and unique resource page, specifically designed for beginners. We take pride in being a supportive and knowledgeable community for anyone interested in Boer goats. .
