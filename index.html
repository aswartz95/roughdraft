About Us

We are a small family-owned business dedicated to breeding high-quality Boer goats. Our goal is to provide the best experience for our showmen and support our buyers through every step of their journey.

Show Know

Showing goats at stock shows requires hard work and dedication. Let us support you on this journey by sharing valuable tips and tricks we've learned along the way. Our goal is to help you become the best showman you can be.

Goats for Sale

We have a variety of Boer goats available for sale or lease. Each goat can be registered for a small fee, and all sales include a vet health certificate. Check our listings for the latest availability.

Our Services

We offer a range of services including leasing programs, clinics for show preparation, and ongoing support for our customers. Our lease program is unique and involves a contract detailing the responsibilities of the lessee.

Contact us to schedule a visit or consultation. We are here to help you with all your Boer goat showing needs.
